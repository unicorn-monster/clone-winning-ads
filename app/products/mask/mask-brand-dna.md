## 02 — Visual System

### Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Pitch Black (background, device casing) | `#0A0A0A` |
| **Device Body** | Matte Device Black | `#1C1C1E` |
| **Secondary** | Warm Off-White (skin, text, clean bg) | `#F5F0EB` |
| **Signature Accent** | LED Red (glow halos, CTAs, UI) | `#E8391A` |
| **Tertiary Accent** | Warm Amber (610nm wavelength visuals) | `#F0A860` |
| **Mode Accent** | Blue LED (460nm acne mode) | `#3A7BDC` |
| **NIR Accent** | NIR Pink (850nm deep repair) | `#FF6B9D` |

### Typography System

**Primary Typeface — Headlines**
- Extra-bold geometric sans-serif (approx. Neue Haas Grotesk / Inter Tight / custom)
- Weight: 700–900
- Case: ALL-CAPS for hero benefit statements
- Tracking: tight (−0.02em to −0.03em)
- Usage: hero headlines, product names, stat callouts

**Secondary Typeface — Body & Labels**
- Clean light sans-serif (Shopify default / Helvetica-adjacent)
- Weight: 300–400
- Line-height: generous (1.7–1.8)
- Tracking: normal
- Usage: product descriptions, FAQs, spec copy

**Tertiary — Mono / Labels**
- Monospaced (DM Mono or similar)
- Usage: spec numbers, wavelength labels, badge text, eyebrow labels
- Letter-spacing: wide (0.2–0.3em), always uppercase

**Distinctive treatments:**
- Large numerics used as display anchors: **272**, **105mW/cm²**, **850nm**
- Strikethrough pricing always present: ~~$299.99~~ → **$139.99**

### CTA Color & Style

Black fill with white text, or bold red pill on dark background. High-contrast, sharp edges. No pastel or gradient CTA buttons.

### Layout Density

- **Above the fold:** Airy — large product hero, minimal text, generous whitespace
- **Below the fold:** Dense — icon grids, comparison tables, spec breakdowns, FAQ accordions
- **Structure:** Grid-based throughout, not organic

## 03 — Photography Direction

### Lighting

Studio strobe, often front-lit or slightly top-lit for model shots. Product flats use pure white infinity or deep black sweep. The mask's red LEDs are **always illuminated** in lifestyle shots — the red/rose glow on skin is the brand's single most consistent visual signature.

### Color Grading

High contrast. Skin tones warm but not saturated. The LED glow is boosted in post — a soft red-rose halo radiating from the mask onto the face/neck. Black backgrounds are crushed to true black. No vintage film tones, no desaturation.

### Composition

- **Product hero:** Centered, large, isolated on black or white
- **Model lifestyle:** Face or face+neck only, often 3/4 angle
- **Before/after:** Clinical side-by-side cropping
- **Flat-lay:** Dark slate surface, product + accessories neatly arranged
- No kitchen scenes, no outdoor lifestyle, no environmental storytelling

### Subject Matter

Female subject, approximately 30–45, ethnically diverse, clear skin. Mask always worn and **LED-lit**. Close-up face shots. Some aspirational "after" shots with glowing, dewy skin. Doctor advisor portraits for clinical credibility.

### Props & Surfaces

- Dark matte stone or slate surfaces
- White infinity sweep for clinical/product shots
- Magnetic battery pack always visible alongside mask
- Minimal props — no florals, no candles, no clutter
- Occasional: white towel, robe, bathroom vanity edge

### Mood

Clinical luxury. Serious but accessible. "This actually works" energy. Not aspirational spa fantasy — more **confident, modern self-care ritual**. The glowing red mask is the aesthetic icon.

### ⚠️ Signature Visual Element

> The mask's LEDs **must be illuminated** in all lifestyle photography. The warm red/rose glow falling across the wearer's face is INIA's single most recognisable visual element. This red-light halo — casting crimson-to-amber across cheekbones and forehead — is the brand's hero aesthetic moment and must be present or referenced in all AI-generated images.

## 05 — Ad Creative Style

### Typical Formats

| Format | Description |
|---|---|
| **Hero Product** | Illuminated mask on pure black/white background. Bold stat callout. Clean, no clutter. |
| **UGC / Testimonial** | Real-user or influencer selfie wearing lit mask. Quote overlay. Authentic feel + branded end card. |
| **Before / After** | Clinical split-screen. Subdued before vs. glowing after. Week markers in mono font. White background. |
| **Benefit Carousel** | Multi-slide: 1 wavelength = 1 slide. Dark bg. Icon + wavelength + benefit. Red → Blue → NIR. |
| **Spec Comparison** | Table ad: INIA vs "Other Brands." Green checkmarks + bolded specs. Competitor column vague/inferior. |
| **Lifestyle In-Use** | Model wearing lit mask while relaxing. Caption: "10 minutes while you relax." Emphasises effortlessness. |

### Text Overlay Style

Bold sans-serif, white on dark. Short punchy headlines. **Metric-forward** ("272 LEDs", "105mW/cm²", "50% off"). Discount framing prominent — strikethrough original price always shown.

### Photo vs. Illustration

Primarily photo-based. Clinical diagrams/illustrations used for wavelength penetration explainers (skin-layer cross-section diagrams). No hand-drawn illustration. No lifestyle illustration.

### UGC Usage

Heavy UGC presence on TikTok and Facebook. Unboxing content, first-impression reviews, before/after self-filmed. Brand reposts and incorporates into ad creative.

### Offer Presentation

Aggressive discount display — $299.99 struck through → $139.99. Trust badges: "90-day Risk-free Return", "2-Year Warranty", "FDA-Cleared", "Clinically Proven". Urgency without overt countdown timers.
