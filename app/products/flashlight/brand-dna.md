# Dinosaurized — Brand DNA Document

**Site:** dinosaurized.com · **Category:** Tactical Gear & Holsters · **Model:** DTC / Shopify

---

## Brand Overview

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Name / Tagline** | Dinosaurized — "An Army Store" / "Top Tactical Gears – Survival Tools" |
| **Design Agency** | None identified. In-house / bootstrapped. Founders: James & Vo, Texas City TX veterans. |
| **Positioning** | Value-first DTC tactical gear for everyday concealed carriers, gun owners, truck/cab drivers, and 2A enthusiasts. Emphasizes body comfort, back health, and concealment — not operator aesthetics. |
| **Competitive Differentiation** | Wins on relatable copy ("fat guy holster"), aggressive scarcity offers, and niche pain-point targeting (seated draw, plus-size carry, back pain). Not aspirational military — everyman tactical. |

**Voice Adjectives:** `Blunt` · `Folksy` · `Urgency-driven` · `Patriotic` · `Problem-obsessed`

### Typography

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| **Primary Font** | Bold condensed sans-serif — likely **Oswald Bold** or similar (Barlow Condensed Bold, Anton). All-caps or large-cap headlines. |
| **Secondary Font** | Readable system sans (Roboto / Open Sans / system-ui). Body copy, FAQs, product descriptions. |
| **Headline Style** | Heavy weight, tight tracking, often all-caps or title-case. Scarcity callouts use bold italic or colored text. |
| **CTA Style** | High-contrast button — amber yellow (`#DD9933`) fill with white text, rounded corners (~8px radius). All-caps bold label: "ADD TO CART". Selected variant states also use `#DD9933`. Unselected variants: white fill with grey border. |
| **Layout Density** | Dense. Long-form product pages, stacked sections, minimal negative space. Feature bullets + story paragraphs + FAQ + reviews in one scroll. |

## Photography Direction

| Dimension | Spec |
|---|---|
| **Lighting** | Flat studio or natural outdoor light. No dramatic side-lighting. White/grey cyclorama for products. Lifestyle: overcast daylight or indoor ambient. Minimal shadows. |
| **Color Grading** | Muted, desaturated warm tones. Military-adjacent palette: tans, olives, blacks, greys. No heavy Instagram filters. |
| **Composition** | Centered product on white/grey background (hero shots). On-body lifestyle shots show product worn under shirt or jacket. Flat-lays with supporting gear for collection shots. |
| **Subject Matter** | Holsters worn on torso. Products laid flat or hanging. No weapons in use. Occasional male model (everyday build, 30–55, not operator physique). Gear shown in daily-life settings (car interior, desk, casual wear). |
| **Props & Surfaces** | White seamless. Concrete or wood surfaces. Work belt, tactical pen, carabiner, keychain multitool as supporting props. No military staging (sandbags, helmets, camo netting). |
| **Mood** | Utilitarian. Matter-of-fact. "This is a real product for real people." Not cinematic, not aspirational. Closer to Amazon product page than REI campaign. |

## Product Details (Core: Holsters)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Materials** | Neoprene (belly bands), premium cowhide leather (Gut Guardian), FlexForce™ spandex (Dinosaur 3-in-1), Kydex polymer (hard-shell line). Black, brown, and tan colorways. |
| **Physical Form** | Belly-band / shoulder-strap / waistband form factors. Adjustable velcro closures. Multi-pouch systems. Ambidextrous. Matte/textile surface finish (not glossy). |
| **Branding on Product** | Minimal. Small "Dinosaurized" text label or subtle dinosaur silhouette logo on strap or interior tag. Logo: simplified raptor/dino silhouette + bold wordmark. |
| **Distinctive Features** | Extra magazine pouches. Phone pockets. Breathable mesh backing. Slim concealment profile. Products named after body concerns: "FatGuy," "Gut Guardian," "Baldman." |
| **Packaging** | Minimal. Generic brown/white poly mailer or plain cardboard box. No premium unboxing. Folded product in poly bag with small instruction card. Utilitarian. |

## Ad Creative Style

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Formats** | Static single image · Carousel (problem → solution) · Short-form video (TikTok/Reels) · Long-form VSL on product page |
| **Text Overlay** | Bold headline overlay, high contrast (white on dark). Problem-statement hooks: *"TRADITIONAL HOLSTERS FAIL IN WINTER"* / *"YOUR HOLSTER IS HURTING YOUR BACK."* Large discount % overlays. |
| **Photo vs Illustration** | 100% photography. No illustration or AI imagery. Heavy product lifestyle photography (on-body). Infographics used on product pages to explain fit. |
| **UGC Usage** | Yes — customer review quotes prominently featured on product pages and likely in ads. Review screenshots and star ratings as social proof. No polished influencer content. |
| **Offer Presentation** | Aggressive. "ONE-TIME 50% OFF." Scarcity: *"Stock flying off shelves."* FOMO-driven urgency copy. Discount badge overlaid on product image. Countdown timers implied. |

## Competitive Context

| Competitor | Visual Differentiation |
|---|---|
| **5.11 Tactical** | Premium brand, professional positioning (law enforcement, military). High-production photography, clean minimalist layout, aspirational tone, price premium. Dinosaurized is the value/everyman alternative — less aspirational, more problem-solving. |
| **Alien Gear Holsters** | U.S.-made, feature-focused hard-shell holsters. Similar DTC model but more premium brand identity. Dinosaurized differentiates with soft/belly-band focus, body-positive naming, and aggressive discounting. |
| **Concealment Express / KYDEX IWB sellers** | Functional, price-competitive. Dinosaurized wins by solving niche pain points (plus-size, seated carry, back pain) with named product lines and longer-form persuasive copy. |
