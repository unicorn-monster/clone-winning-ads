# Interactive Dog Ball Toy

## Overview

An interactive, automatic moving dog toy designed for boredom relief, exercise, and training. Features a wicked ball mechanism with USB rechargeable battery and Bluetooth connectivity.

**Price:** $19.99 (was $19.99)

## Key Features

- **3 Intelligent Working Modes**
  - Fast Mode – moves on thin carpet and hard floor
  - Interactive Mode – activates only when touched
  - Slow Mode – hard floor only

- **Sensor Chip** – detects touch and environment
- **LED Lights** – built-in lighting effects
- **Touch Activated** – responds to pet interaction
- **USB Charging** – rechargeable, approximately 3 hours for a full charge
- **Playable Audio** – emits sounds to engage pets
- **Bluetooth Connection** – connect via app for additional control
