## FAQs & Warranty

**Can the wireless red light mask be used on all skin types?**
Yes. The INIA GLOW Wireless Red Light Therapy Mask is safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin, with no adverse side effects and no pain. It is certified as an FDA Class II medical device and CE medical device, with proven effectiveness validated by third-party organizations like SGS.

**How does INIA GLOW wireless red light mask work?**
INIA's red light therapy products utilize advanced LED phototherapy, a technology widely trusted by dermatologists. The mask emits clinically proven wavelengths — red (630nm), blue (460nm), yellow (590nm), and near-infrared (850nm) — to trigger beneficial biochemical reactions, enhancing skin recovery, boosting cell function, and promoting healing, resulting in firmer, cleaner, and rejuvenated skin.

**Is it really safe to use LED technology near my eyes?**
Absolutely. The INIA GLOW Wireless Mask is FDA-cleared, meaning it meets safety standards and can be used without protective goggles. The mask is designed to lie directly on your skin, and its LED lights are placed at a clinically safe distance from the eye area. Protective eye pads are included for added comfort.

**Does the number of lamp beads matter in a red light therapy mask?**
Yes. LED density is key to delivering effective results. The INIA GLOW uses 272 medical-grade LEDs (68 bulbs with 4 LEDs each) to deliver a powerful irradiance of 50mW/cm². This array enables red, blue, yellow, and near-infrared wavelengths to penetrate deeply, stimulating ATP regeneration in skin cells, which helps address issues like irritation, rosacea, redness, and acne. Many budget devices may have numerous LEDs but often lack sufficient irradiance, limiting therapeutic effects.

**Why combine with near-infrared light therapy?**
Near-infrared light promotes deep stimulation of collagen and elastin production, promoting natural repair and reducing wrinkles. It complements red light's surface benefits, maximizing overall skin and tissue health for a comprehensive anti-aging and repair solution.
> Note: NIR wavelengths ≥800nm are invisible to the eye, so red light devices without NIR are often less effective.

**What is the red light therapy mask recommended usage frequency?**
The INIA GLOW wireless red light therapy mask is designed for safe daily use. For best results, follow the usage frequency specified in the user manual. Regular use, as advised, can help achieve and maintain optimal results without overexposure.

**Is it possible to use the wireless red light therapy mask while it is charging?**
No. The remote control needs to be disconnected from the charger to operate the mask properly. It is essential to use the mask unplugged to ensure safety and proper functionality.

**How should I clean the red light therapy mask?**
Wipe the mask with a damp cloth, then dry it thoroughly. Avoid abrasive or chemical cleansers. Eye pads can be removed and washed separately.
