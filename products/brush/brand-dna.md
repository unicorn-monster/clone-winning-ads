## Image Generation Prompt Modifier

> Shot in the visual style of the Maximutt MaxPro 2-in-1 product page: warm natural indoor or backyard light, slightly warm-neutral grade. Primary accent colors are teal-blue (#2A7BC4, #5BB8D4) from the product itself and a vibrant green (#3CB55A) for CTA and action elements. White page background, black text. Subjects are large double-coated dog breeds (Husky, Golden Retriever, GSD) being groomed by a human hand holding a teal-blue brush. Visible fur removal and relaxed dog expressions. Composition is hands-on and action-oriented — grooming in progress, not posed. UGC-authentic framing: slightly loose, real-home environments (lawn, hardwood, couch). No gradients, no studio lighting, no sterile backgrounds. Mood is satisfying, practical, and warm.
